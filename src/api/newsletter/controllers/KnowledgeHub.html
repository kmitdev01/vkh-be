The Sustainable Energy for All Knowledge Hub is an interactive platform providing sustainable energy

We, at SEforALL in partnership with the Rockefeller foundation and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Italy, strive to improve the user friendliness and our database to include the best-in-class resources in the field of electricity access.

If you wish to recommend a resource that you feel should be featured on the Sustainable Energy for All Knowledge Hub, please contact us at KnowledgeHub@seforall.org.

Stay tuned for additional pillars of sustainable energy - access to clean cooking, sustainable cooling, energy efficiency and renewable energy - which will be added soon.
